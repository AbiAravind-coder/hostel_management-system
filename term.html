Rules and Regulations:

  • 1. Students must attend all classes regularly.
  • 2. Use of mobile phones during class hours is prohibited.
  • 3. Visitors are only allowed during designated visiting hours.
  • 4. Smoking and consumption of alcohol or drugs is strictly forbidden.
  • 5. Curfew timings must be strictly adhered to.
  • 6. Noise levels must be kept low to respect the study environment.
  • 7. Unauthorized absence from the hostel requires prior approval.
  • 8. Students must carry their ID cards at all times within the hostel premises.
  • 9. Respect for teachers, fellow students, and hostel staff is mandatory.
  • 10. Maintain cleanliness in personal spaces and common areas.
  • 11. Any misconduct or unruly behavior will result in strict disciplinary action.
  • 12. Students are responsible for the maintenance of their rooms and hostel property.
  • 13. Reporting damages or maintenance needs promptly is mandatory.
  • 14. Students must adhere to fire safety and security protocols.
  • 15. The use of electrical appliances like heaters is prohibited unless permitted.
  • 16. Students are responsible for locking their rooms when leaving.
  • 17. Hosting unauthorized individuals or guests overnight is prohibited.
  • 18. Students must comply with payment deadlines for hostel fees.
  • 19. Littering or improper waste disposal is strictly prohibited.
  • 20. Acts of vandalism or theft will lead to immediate termination of stay.
